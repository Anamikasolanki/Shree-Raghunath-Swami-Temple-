ॐ कृष्णाय वासुदेवाय हरये परमात्मने ।
प्रणतः क्लेशनाशाय गोविंदाय नमो नमः ।।
1000-900 years old Shri Raghunath Swami Temple, whose historical footprints are according to popular belief, it is said that the idol of this temple was not made by hands but was descended from the ground.To understand the incident of incarnation of this idol, we will have to go back hundreds of years. Presently this temple is situated in Bhanpura tehsil of Mandsaur district. Yes, this is the same Bhanpura which was the capital of Holkar dynasty. Bhanpura was the capital of Holkar dynasty during the reign of Maharaj Shri Yashwant Rao Holkar between 1811 and 1833.After his death, Indore was made the capital by his successor. Today's Bhanpura used to be in Indargarh 1000 to 900 years ago.Which was a settlement located one kilometer away from present Bhanpura. There used to be an ashram of a great ascetic sage near this settlement. Whose food his disciples used to bring as alms from this colony. But this alms used to come from only two family houses. This family had two houses, one belonging to Rathore and one belonging to Prajapati community. Only two handfuls of grains from each house were used for food. One day when the same sage asked his disciples the reason for getting less alms, the disciples told him that they got alms from both the worlds. Hearing this, the sage got angry and cursed that despite so many houses in the settlement, only two houses were getting alms and ordered to vacate the settlement.
And due to this curse, the sage caused chaos to that land with his divine power. The evidence of which is still buried hundreds of feet deep in the ground even today. Which proves this incident.there used to be a pond from where the idol descended, which had dried up due to the curse and the people of the village settled on the banks of this pond. In which those two families were also included. Who used to give alms. And at that time by Ghanavar Bhil when When digging for the construction of a hut, during the digging, Bhil's tool collided with the stone of the idol and got clogged but he could not remove that stone. That night, a member of the same Rathore family named Raghunath Rathore. He had a vision of Lord Vishnu in his dream and saw the idol coming out and Talked about installing the statue. The next day, Raghunath Rathore took out the idol which was made of black and black stone. Along with the idol, two pots of gold were found. In which one pitcher was kept below the installation site of the idol and with the help of the other pitcher the temple was constructed and with the passage of time the construction of this temple has been going on. so temple construction The person who established the temple came to know the idol by the name of Shri Raghunath Rathore and the same temple is still being operated by the Ghanavar Rathore Teli Samaj.
There are so many miracles of this temple that it will not be easy to tell them all. Today we will definitely publish an incident related to this miracle because the family generation of the person associated with this incident is still present. This person himself worshiped Shri Raghunath Swami. And every morning the Lord used to give him a gold coin when he opened the door of the temple. In 1956, when scarlet fever broke out, a famine-like situation arose but this did not affect the priest's daily routine due to which people accused the priest of theft. And due to fear of complaint in the police station,he told people about the coin given by Lord Shri Raghunath Swami, after that the priest stopped getting the coin. The generation of priests still exists today. Who have been telling about this miracle.
The second miracle which we call not a miracle but a matter of justice. Yes, this temple is called the temple of justice by the Holkar state itself. Even today, when the court fails to hear any case, this temple solves that case in 8 days.For this reason Lord Shri Raghunath Swami is also called the city judge.
Recently, when the restoration work of the temple was going on under the guidance of the then president of Rathore society, Shri Mangal Singh Ji Rathore and priest Shri Santosh Ji Sharma That's why they got to see this miracle not once but twice or thrice.A miracle in which when at around 3 o'clock in the night the laborers wanted to drink tea, the shops were not open and tea was not available even milk was not available at priest home also then a cow without a calf was found to come there and keeping the head of the cow in the lap of Mangal ji Rathod and after some time the priest Shri Santosh ji taking out a tumbler of milk from the breasts and the cow disappearing is a miracle of God. Meanwhile, there was another incident in which around 10 to 20 laborers working on the roof of the temple during the construction work of the temple, after they got down, the roof collapsed and the lives of the laborers were saved.After finding out the reason for the incident, everyone was surprised; in that incident, the load of the entire roof along with the weight of the workers was being borne by just one pebble of the lantern and the roof collapsed after the workers got down. This is nothing less than a miracle.Therefore, many such miracles are performed by God every moment. They are created somewhere and fulfill the wishes of the devotee.
